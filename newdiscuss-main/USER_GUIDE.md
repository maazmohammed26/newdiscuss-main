# User Guide: New Features

Welcome to the updated Discuss platform! Here's how to use all the new features.

## 🎨 Switching to the New Discuss Theme

### Step 1: Access Theme Settings
1. Login to your account
2. Click on your profile picture or username in the header
3. Navigate to your **Profile** page

### Step 2: Choose Discuss Theme
1. Scroll to the **"Choose Theme"** section
2. You'll see 3 theme options:
   - **Light** ☀️ - Clean & bright (default)
   - **Dark** 🌙 - Easy on eyes
   - **Discuss** 💻 - Retro terminal (NEW!)

3. Click on the **Discuss** theme card
   - It's highlighted with a "NEW" badge
   - Has a special orange ring around it

### Step 3: Confirm Theme Change
1. A confirmation dialog will appear
2. Read about the Discuss theme features:
   - Retro, tech-inspired design
   - Square edges (no rounded corners)
   - Monospace fonts
   - Terminal-style aesthetics
3. Click **"Apply Discuss Theme"**

### Step 4: Enjoy!
- Your entire app will instantly transform
- Black background with bright green text
- Square corners everywhere
- Monospace font for that classic terminal feel
- Retro button effects with shadows

### Switching Back:
- Simply go back to Profile > Choose Theme
- Select Light or Dark theme
- No confirmation needed when switching away from Discuss theme

## ✏️ Editing Posts & Projects (New Modal Interface)

### The Old Way (Before):
- Edit button opened inline editing
- Content appeared right in the post card
- Limited space, cluttered interface

### The New Way (Now):
- Edit button opens a clean modal
- Full-screen editing experience
- Similar to the "Create Post" modal

### How to Edit:

#### Step 1: Find Your Post
- Go to your Profile page
- Expand the "Your Posts" section
- Or find your post in the Feed

#### Step 2: Click Edit
- Look for the pencil icon (✏️) on your post
- Only visible on posts you created
- Located in the top-right corner

#### Step 3: Edit in Modal
The edit modal will open with:
- **For Projects:**
  - Project Title field
  - Description/Content area
  - GitHub Link field
  - Live Preview Link field
  
- **For Discussions:**
  - Content/Message area

#### Step 4: Save or Cancel
- Click **"Save Changes"** to update your post
- Click **"Cancel"** to discard changes
- Modal will close automatically
- You'll see a success notification

## 🎯 New Features Overview

### 1. Discuss Theme
**What it is:** A retro terminal-style theme with a classic hacker aesthetic.

**Key Features:**
- Black background (#0a0a0a)
- Bright green text (#00FF88)
- Monospace font (Roboto Mono)
- Square corners (no curves)
- Retro button shadows
- Press animations

**Best For:**
- Developers who love terminal aesthetics
- Those who want a unique, stand-out design
- Night-time browsing with character
- Nostalgia for classic computer interfaces

### 2. Modal Editing
**What it is:** A popup window for editing posts instead of inline editing.

**Benefits:**
- More space to work
- Cleaner interface
- Better focus on editing
- Consistent with create post experience
- Easier to see all fields at once

### 3. Theme Selector
**What it is:** A visual way to choose your preferred theme.

**Features:**
- See all 3 themes at once
- Icons and descriptions for each
- Active theme clearly highlighted
- One-click switching
- Confirmation for Discuss theme

## 💡 Tips & Tricks

### Theme Preferences
- **Light Theme**: Best for daytime use, high ambient light
- **Dark Theme**: Easy on eyes, reduces eye strain
- **Discuss Theme**: Maximum style, perfect for screenshots and demos

### Keyboard Shortcuts
While editing in modal:
- `Esc` key closes the modal (cancels edit)
- `Tab` moves between fields
- `Shift + Tab` moves backwards

### Profile Organization
- Use the filter options to find posts by date
- Filter by "This Month", specific month/year
- Toggle "Your Posts" section to hide/show

## ❓ FAQ

### Q: Can I switch themes anytime?
**A:** Yes! Go to Profile > Choose Theme and select any theme instantly.

### Q: Will my theme choice affect other users?
**A:** No, your theme choice is personal and only affects your view.

### Q: Does the Discuss theme work on mobile?
**A:** Absolutely! It works perfectly on all devices.

### Q: Can I edit posts from the feed or only from my profile?
**A:** You can edit from anywhere - feed, profile, or post detail pages.

### Q: Is there a dark version of the Discuss theme?
**A:** The Discuss theme IS a dark theme! It's black with green text.

### Q: Can I customize the Discuss theme colors?
**A:** Not currently, but the green-on-black is the authentic terminal experience!

### Q: Will more themes be added?
**A:** Possibly! Let us know what themes you'd like to see.

### Q: Does editing in a modal support hashtags?
**A:** Yes! All features work in the edit modal, though hashtags can't be changed.

## 🛠️ Troubleshooting

### Theme Not Changing:
1. Try refreshing the page (Ctrl+R or Cmd+R)
2. Clear browser cache
3. Make sure you clicked "Apply Discuss Theme" in the confirmation

### Edit Modal Not Opening:
1. Make sure you're logged in
2. Ensure it's your own post (edit button only shows on your posts)
3. Try refreshing the page

### Discuss Theme Looks Wrong:
1. Check if you have any browser extensions blocking styles
2. Try disabling ad blockers temporarily
3. Ensure your browser is up to date
